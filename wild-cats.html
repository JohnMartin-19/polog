Types of wild cats

There are many types of wild cats. As we all know from our general knowledge the wild means not tamed at home. Therefore these wild cats live in the wilderness and are adapted to different climatic regions of the world

Here are some type of wild cats

  • Tiger
  • Lion
  • Puma
  • Jaguar

These wild cats belong to the same genus but different species. They however, communicate with each other via different sounds that we as humans cannot understand
